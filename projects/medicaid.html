Medicaid Provider Contributions in USA's COVID-19 Claims

  • Tech Stack: Python, Tableau
  • Tableau URL: Project Link

Welcome to my individual project, where I delve into the impactful role of Medicaid providers in the fight against COVID-19 across the United States. Through an interactive dashboard meticulously crafted in Tableau, I offer a detailed exploration of the distribution and efficacy of funds allocated for testing, treatment, and vaccination efforts. As you navigate through my dashboard, you'll uncover a wealth of insights designed to illuminate the tireless efforts of healthcare providers nationwide.

Embark on a journey through the intricacies of COVID-19 relief programs, as my dashboard unveils the geographic footprint of testing, treatment, and vaccination initiatives across the nation. From bustling metropolises to tranquil countrysides, every corner of the USA is represented, showcasing the collective resolve to confront the pandemic head-on. Dive deeper into the data as you explore the top 50 providers championing the cause, their dedication exemplified through significant contributions to testing, treatment, and vaccination campaigns. With funding stemming from legislative acts like the American Rescue Plan Act and the Provider Relief Fund, these healthcare heroes have emerged as pillars of resilience in the face of adversity.

Join me in honoring and acknowledging the unwavering commitment of healthcare providers in safeguarding public health amidst the COVID-19 crisis.
